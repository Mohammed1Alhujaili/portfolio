AI-generated deepfakes are increasingly indistinguishable from real footage, posing serious threats to digital trust, journalism, and legal systems.

Solution

End-to-end forensics pipeline: face localisation via RetinaFace → binary classification via fine-tuned XceptionNet → spatial explainability via Grad-CAM heatmaps.
